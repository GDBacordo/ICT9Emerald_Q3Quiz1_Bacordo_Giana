A set of circumstances makes Anna Scott, a famous actress, fall in love with William Thacker, owner of a bookstore in Notting Hill. But the paparazzi's fascination with her complicates their bond. Hugh Grant as William Thacker and Julia Roberts as Anna Scott

Directed by Roger Michell
Written by Richard Curtis

Duration: 2 hours 4 minutes		 Notting Hill beautifully portays the love between a top Hollywood actress and a small bookshop owner in London. It shows how differences in social status cannot conquer the power of true love. Their love story is inspiring, moving, and an overall enjoyable watch.
Two North Carolina teens, Landon Carter and Jamie Sullivan, are thrown together after Landon gets into trouble and is sentenced to perform community service. Mandy Moore as Jamie Sullivan and Shane West as Landon Carter

Directed by Adam Shankman
Written by Nicholas Sparks and Karen Janszen

Duration: 1 hour and 41 minutes		 A Walk to Remember is a heart-wrenching movie about two teenagers, one who learns how to improve himself and one who is gone too soon. This movie teaches us many lessons in love and how to be a better person, all taught by the main character Jamie and her love interest Landon. This movie is a valuable watch that will most definitely leave you crying.
